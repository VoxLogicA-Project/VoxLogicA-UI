Workspace Management

Save and organize your work in workspaces: they will preserve your analysis setup, scripts, and visualizations.

Dataset & Case Management

Efficiently browse your imaging datasets. Search your cases, and quickly locate that one case that obtained the best DICE value!

Multiplanar Visualization

View and analyze neuroimaging data from multiple angles with synchronized cross-referencing. Powered by Niivue, switch between axial, sagittal, coronal, and 3D views.

Synchronized cross-referencing
Interactive 3D visualization

Layer Management

Control multiple imaging layers with precise opacity and color mapping tools. Show or hide layers to focus on specific aspects of your analysis.

Toggle layer visibility
Adjust layer opacity

Multi-Case Visualization

View and analyze multiple cases simultaneously with synchronized controls and comparisons.

// Background removal
load flair = "flair.nii.gz"

let brain = complement (background)
